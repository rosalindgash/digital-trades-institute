# **LEVEL 3: Advanced AI Mastery Certificate**

**Purpose:** Equip individuals with high‑level, professional AI capabilities across prompting, workflow automation, complex reasoning, and safety/oversight.

**Format:** Silent, slide‑based micro‑lessons (250–450 words each), modular, licensing‑friendly, reusable.

# **Module 1 — Advanced Prompt Engineering & Model Steering**

**Competency Goal:** Demonstrate precise control over model behavior across tasks, domains, and constraints.

### **Module Overview**

* How advanced prompting differs from basic prompting  
* Model steering frameworks  
* Constraint design and multi‑step reasoning  
* Debugging model behavior  
* Designing prompts for reliability, not cleverness

### **Micro‑Lesson Map**

1. **Mental Models of LLM Behavior**  
2. **Prompt Structures for Precision & Control**  
3. **Constraint‑Driven Prompting (Rules, Formats, Guardrails)**  
4. **Multi‑Step Reasoning & Chain‑of‑Thought Scaffolds**  
5. **Role, Style, and Domain Conditioning**  
6. **Debugging & Refining Model Outputs**  
7. **Prompt Patterns for Complex Tasks**  
8. **Evaluating Prompt Quality & Failure Modes**
